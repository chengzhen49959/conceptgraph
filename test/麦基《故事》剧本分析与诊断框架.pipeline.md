### Non-event  x2 [new]  · aliases: Non-event scene

### Dramatic Action  x2 [judged-distinct]  · aliases: Action, Behavior on Screen
Dramatic action is purposeful behavior in which a character acts toward a goal while facing obstacles, risk, or opposition. Coincidence resolution is a narrative technique in which accidental or coincidental events solve the central dramatic problem rather than character decisions, actions, or causal development.
